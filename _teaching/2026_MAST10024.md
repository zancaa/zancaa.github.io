This subject is a first-year undergraduate core elective for the Bachelor of Biomedicine (and also available as breadth for other degrees). In Semester 1, 2026, there are approximately 100 enrolments. The course covers the mathematics behind mass action kinetics, enzyme kinetics, hypothesis testing, linear regression and epidemic modelling. My contributions to the material have been to update assessment (including adding a 'Part A' to every assignment, asking students to reflect on course materials), add new tutorial questions and reorder material to be better aligned, and create new revision materials.
