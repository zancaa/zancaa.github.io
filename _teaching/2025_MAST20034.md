This is a second-year undergraduate statistics subject that is a core subject for the Business Analytics major in the Bachelor of Commerce. This subject ran for the first time in Semester 2, 2025. I co-coordinated with Dr. Paul Fijn. The course content includes study design and best practice for collecting data, working with and reporting on data and interpreting reports of data. We focussed on giving students agency with their in-class tasks and assessment. This involved creating a collection of case studied for students to choose from (supported by funding from a Teaching Support Scheme Grant). Paul and I are very interested in Universal Design for Learning and applied many of these principles to the design and implementation of the subject. Feel free to ask me more about this!
