Adriana (Age, pronounced 'aij') Zanca is a Research Fellow in Modelling Biological Heterogeneity in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at The University of Melbourne. Age's research interests span mathematics education and mathematical and theoretical biology. She _adores_ making a fool of herself (i.e. public speaking) and believes that mathematics should be for _everyone_. For more information about Age and her work, see her [Teaching](https://zancaa.github.io/teaching), [Publications](https://zancaa.github.io/publications), [Talks](https://zancaa.github.io/talks), and [Everything else!](https://zancaa.github.io/portfolio) pages :blush:.
