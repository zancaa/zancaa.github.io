--- title: "Mentoring" excerpt: "Mentoring" collection: portfolio --- I have participated in various formal and informal mentoring programs (as mentor and mentee), including, but not limited to, programs run by the Society for Mathematical Biology, the Women in Mathematics Special Interest Group (a special interest group of the Australian Mathematical Society), and several program run within The University of Melbourne.
