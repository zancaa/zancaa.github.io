I spoke with Dr Shane Huntington on the RRR radio program, [Einstein A-Go-Go on the 11th of April, 2021](https://www.rrr.org.au/explore/programs/einstein-a-go-go/episodes/15685-einstein-a-go-go-11-april-2021). I was speaking about working as a casual staff member, while also being a student at the university I was working at, and the tension between the two roles.
