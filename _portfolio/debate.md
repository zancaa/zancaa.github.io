In 2025, I was invited to be a debate speaker for the Australian Mathematical Society's debate (brought back in 2025 after being retired for a number of years). The topic was, 'will AI kill mathematics?' and I was on the affirmative team, alongside Professor Florian Breuer and Associate Professor Adam Piggott. Despite being on the inevitable losing side, this was a wonderful experience and I was grateful for the opportunity to bring some fun to conference with some charm and flair. Thank you to Professor Marcel Jackson for organising and hosting the debate and for the invitation to speak.
